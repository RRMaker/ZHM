Zebrafish GenetiX

We are Zebrafish GenetiX, a team of 3 students from the University of North Texas who came together to solve a major problem in zebrafish hemostasis research. Recently, we all joined the Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science @ UNT, an early-college program for high-schoolers, which offers college-level courses, motivated UNT communities, and real science research opportunities.

Through this program, we got to know each other well and realized how serious the problems in current zebrafish hemostasis research were. Combined with our complementary skills, that realization led us to form this team. (Click the images)
